About Valhalla:

The sandwiches and burgers are a fusion of hearty sandwich favorites with a Scandinavian cuisine twist. Some of the ingredients chosen derive from theories of what food Vikings ate while still creating delectable sandwiches for all to enjoy. For example, smoked fish, cheeses and barley & rye bread and using honey as a sweetener. Moreover the goal of the restaurant is to keep ingredients homemade and local, such as making homemade breads, using local cheese companies organic garden fresh vegetables. We proudly support our local agriculture.
