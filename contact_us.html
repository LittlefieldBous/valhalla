Viking Trivia:
Where did the word Ahoy originate?

The Answer: The exact origins of the word "ahoy" are unknown. Only theories. One theory is that "ahoy" derived from the Old Norse greeting, "Heill" then became "hail" which eventually became "ahoy in Middle English. Consequently Bell's preferred way to answer the phone was "ahoy-hoy"
